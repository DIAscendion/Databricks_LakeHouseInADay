# Databricks Gold Aggregated Transformation Data Mapping

## Overview
This document details the data mapping, aggregation logic, validation rules, and cleansing mechanisms for the Aggregated Tables in the Gold Layer of the TMS Shipment Application. It ensures that summary metrics are accurately calculated from the Silver Layer, supporting advanced analytics and business reporting. The mapping covers the transformation of cleansed, conformed Silver data into curated, aggregated Gold tables, with a focus on metric-level accuracy, data quality, and business rule compliance.

## Explanations for Complex Transformations and Business Rules
- **Time Bucketization**: All aggregations are grouped by day (for daily summary) or month (for monthly performance) using DATE_TRUNC functions.
- **Status Mapping**: Shipment status and cancellation/reconciliation flags are mapped using code tables and business rules to ensure consistency.
- **Unit Conversion**: Distance fields are checked for unit consistency; if mixed, all values are converted to a standard unit (e.g., miles).
- **Percentage and Ratio Calculations**: All percentage fields are calculated with denominator validation to avoid division by zero and are rounded to two decimals.
- **Data Cleansing**: Outliers (e.g., out_of_route_distance > distance) are flagged, and nulls are handled as per business rules.
- **Joins to Dimensions**: Surrogate keys (carrier_key, facility_key) are derived via joins to dimension tables for referential integrity.
- **Validation Rules**: All mandatory fields are checked for nulls, uniqueness, and valid domain values as per constraints file.
